ABOUT US - Best Gym in Town

We started just a few years back with only one thing in mind: DELIVERING FITNESS. We did not associate with any big gym brands or go the way of a franchise model, because business was secondary. Our primary goal was to create the best training environment without any restrictions. Today, we are proud to be called one of the leading names in the gym industry in our city and the best gym in Indore.

We have handpicked the very best equipment for the gym, such as Jerai training machines, Jerai cage for CrossFit, Bull Rock Fitness bumper plates for the deadlift zone, Being Strong cardio equipment, and much more. This attention to detail and quality makes us the best gym in Indore.

Our 3-floor gym facility is designed to provide the best workout experience. Starting with the first floor, we have cardio equipment, changing rooms, and reception. On the second floor, we have our weight training zone with machines targeting every muscle group you can imagine, solidifying our reputation as the best gym in Indore. On the third floor, we boast one of the first and largest CrossFit/functional training zones in the city. This includes activities like tyre flips, kettlebells, bumper plates, a punching bag, a weighted push cart, a training rope, the Jerai cage, and much more, all contributing to our status as the best gym in Indore.
